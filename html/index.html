What The What?

こんにちは！

You are here for one of these two reasons:

You are looking for a highly skilled ninja, who will help you completing a quest you embarked upon. For that scroll down to Find Dat Ninja section and start your search there!

Another reason would be that you want to enter (or amend an entry of) you or someone you know to our ninja database. Scroll down to Manage Dat Ninja to complete your destiny!

Ninja Please! This database is built and managed by the good will of our SSTK community. When adding / updating data, please make sure you are leaving this site in a better shape than you found it. Also, if you find out about a ninja that is missing in our database, that their responsibilities have changed or is no longer with us - please update the database!
